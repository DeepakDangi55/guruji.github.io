THE OLDEST ORGANISED

YOGA INSTITUTE IN THE WORLD.

In 1918, Shri Yogendra ji laid the foundation. His Guruji Shri Madhavdas ji, blessed and trained him to be a true yogi. A Siddha, who demonstrated that being a householder can also enable you to achieve mastery over your physical and mental senses.

Yogendra ji firmly believed that people must be educated about yoga before they start practicing it.
