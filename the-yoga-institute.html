THE YOGA INSTITUTE RISHIKESH.

The Yoga Institute adheres to the tenets of classical yoga, with the objective of making it accessible and relevant to the common man. We take great pride that we have adapted traditional yogic practices to address and resolve the challenges of a global, modern and ever-changing world. We welcome you to join, know more and become a part of an institute with more than ten decades of wisdom, research and practice of yoga. We at Yog Nagri Rishikesh (Yoga capital of the world) welcome every yoga enthusiast to explore authentic traditional yogic practices and learn with best faculties.
